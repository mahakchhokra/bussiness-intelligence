This project presents an interactive **Footwear Sales Overview Dashboard** designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, and market trends across products, regions, states, retailers, and time periods.

### üîç Dashboard Features

* **Dynamic Filters:** Invoice Date, Product, Region, and State for flexible analysis.
* **Sales & Profit Trends:** Monthly comparison of total sales vs. operating profit.
* **Sales Method & Region Analysis:** Breakdown of increases, decreases, and totals by sales channel and region.
* **Product Performance:** Operating profit contribution by product category.
* **Retailer Insights:** Profit distribution across major retailers (e.g., West Gear, Foot Locker, Amazon, Walmart).
* **Geographical Analysis:**

  * Interactive map showing operating profit by state and region.
  * State-level sales performance using a treemap visualization.
  * City-level comparison of total sales and operating profit.
* **Detailed Table View:** Yearly and category-wise breakdown of average price, units sold, total sales, and operating profit.

### üéØ Purpose

The dashboard helps stakeholders:

* Identify top-performing products and regions
* Understand profitability drivers
* Compare retailer and city-level performance
* Support data-driven business and marketing decisions
